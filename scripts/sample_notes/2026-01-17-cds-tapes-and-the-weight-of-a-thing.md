# CDs, tapes, and the weight of a thing

Bought a CD player this week. First time I've owned one since 2008. The thing I forgot about physical media is that it has weight, and weight changes how you relate to it.

A playlist is a current state. An album is a decision someone made once and you experience it again. The decision is the part that matters.
