# Why I keep coming back to quiet games

Played through Outer Wilds again this month. Forgot how much the game trusts the player. There is no hand-holding, no quest log, no waypoints. The game is the player's attention, organized.

That's the design lineage I want Signal Lost to be in. Quiet games that respect attention.
