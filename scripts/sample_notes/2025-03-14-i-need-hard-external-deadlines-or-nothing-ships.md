# I need hard external deadlines or nothing ships

I need hard external deadlines or nothing ships. That's the other side of the same coin. The freedom is real, but so is the silence, and in the silence I write three drafts of a Substack post and publish none of them.

External deadlines force a finish line. The finish line is the actual product. Without it, the work is just rehearsal.
