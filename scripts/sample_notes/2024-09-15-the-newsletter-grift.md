# The newsletter grift

There's a whole economy of people selling courses on how to sell courses. The newsletter grift is upstream of the course grift: build an audience, monetize the audience, then sell them the playbook you used to build it.

Half the Substack creator economy is this. The other half is well-meaning people who are also making a living, which is fine, but the line is thin and the incentives push everyone toward the same kind of content.
