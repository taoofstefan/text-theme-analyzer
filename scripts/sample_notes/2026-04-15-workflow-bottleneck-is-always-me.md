# Workflow bottleneck is always me

Every workflow I design eventually bottlenecks at the human approval step. Not because the human is slow — because the model can't tell what the human would approve without asking.

Two ways out: richer context (so the model can predict approval), or richer defaults (so the human only vetoes, never initiates). Both are real work.
