# The grifter resume

I keep noticing the same resume pattern: someone has launched three or four 'AI startups' in eighteen months, none of which have users, all of which have a Twitter presence, and they're now 'raising a pre-seed round' for the next one.

The tell is the LinkedIn banner — always a gradient, always a vague noun, always a tagline that could mean anything.
